Get to know us

Hi! We are so glad you are here. Daybreakery was opened in 2045 by two best friends, Sena and Semanur, who have dreamed about having their own bakeries since they were in middle school.

We have now become a team of 10 best friends who are all passionate about making people happier with food. All of us have attended top culinary schools to prepare for making our dream come true. We are certain that our fresh baked goods and drinks will add joy to anyone’s day.

Our bakery’s name, Daybreakery, was inspired by our desire to ignite a mindset shift about early mornings. With our food, early mornings will never be miserable. Whether you are on your way to work or school, or just seeking some warmth, stop by Daybreakery!
